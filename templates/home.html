FWI Prediction Model

This project uses machine learning to predict the likelihood and intensity of forest fires based on environmental metrics like temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more. It is designed to support early warning systems by analyzing input data and estimating fire risk.

We trained and evaluated Ridge Regression, Lasso Regression, and ElasticNet models on the dataset.
